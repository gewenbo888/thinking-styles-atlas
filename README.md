# Thinking Styles Atlas · 思维方式图谱

A bilingual single-page comparison of 12 thinking styles. Strict side-by-side English + 中文 throughout. Interactive 6-dimension radar chart, "best style for X" ranker, AI vs Human cognition comparison.

## Coverage

12 thinking styles: Analytical · Critical · Systems · First-Principles · Lateral · Divergent · Convergent · Intuitive · Abstract · Concrete · Probabilistic · Design.

9 sections + extras:
1. Definition — what "thinking style" is and isn't
2. 6 comparison dimensions (logic, creativity, speed, risk, depth, adaptability)
3. **12 detailed style profiles** (definition + strengths/weaknesses bilingual)
4. **Interactive radar chart** — toggle 2-4 styles at once across all 6 dimensions, color-coded
5. One problem, five styles — same business problem solved 5 different ways
6. Decision-making — Kahneman System 1 vs System 2
7. Cognitive biases & limitations table
8. **Hybrid thinking + interactive style ranker** (5 contexts: creative, decision, complex, speed, novel)
9. Human vs AI thinking comparison

## Disclaimer

Sources: Kahneman *Thinking Fast and Slow*, de Bono *Lateral Thinking*, Meadows *Thinking in Systems*, Brown *Change by Design*, Tetlock *Superforecasting*. Profiles educational, not diagnostic.
